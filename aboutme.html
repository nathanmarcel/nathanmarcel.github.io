About Me

Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, I completed my B.S. in mechanical engineering with a minor in computer science from Purdue University in Spring 2022. As an undergraduate student, I applied to be a part of a Combined BSME/MSME 5 year program, in which I took graduate level courses my senior year that would count towards both my undergraduate and graduate degree. As of right now, I am set to graduate with my M.S. in mechanical engineering with a concentration in robotics in Spring 2023.

I am passionate about robotic systems, especially those with medical applications. I have had two previous internships working on two different surgical robots (J&J's Monarch Platform and Stryker's MAKO Knee & Hip Replacement Arm). At these internships, I gained practical experience with control systems and mechanical design, such as programming controllers to evaluate the performance and flexibility of a robot with Python, as well as designing, fabricating, and then printing a 6-link robotic manipulator using Solidworks, encoders and actuators, and used MATLAB for control.
